Skate City

JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, HTML5, CSS3, WeChat

Discover your local skatespots in Shanghai.
Users can find, create and share skatespots with their WeChat contacts.


Skate City was created during my time at Le Wagon Shanghai. I was part of a team of 4 and my role was the front end developer. The video below was part of Demo Day where we pitched our apps to industry experts, family and friends.

There were many challenges as we attempted to navigate WeChat Mini Programs for the first time. One of the features I worked on was creating the filter tags with JavaScript so users could select and browse different skate styles.

To the left is an updated version with a my own UI design as I wanted the design to look more fresh and modern.
